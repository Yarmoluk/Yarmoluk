## The Thesis

A single architecture — decompose any domain into structured concept graphs, map relationships, compress for LLM reasoning, deploy. Works regardless of field: oral surgery, cybersecurity, peptide science, IP law, defense contracting, pharmaceutical regulation, congressional trading.

**The methodology is domain-agnostic. Graphify.md is the productization.**

## Graphify.md — Market Intelligence Infrastructure

> *LLM-ready market intelligence from public data — 10x cheaper AI.*

**[graphify-site](https://github.com/Yarmoluk/graphify-site)** · **[graphifymd](https://github.com/Yarmoluk/graphifymd)** · **[graphifymd-signal](https://github.com/Yarmoluk/graphifymd-signal)**

Compressed Knowledge Graph files from six free public sources — SEC EDGAR, GDELT, USPTO, USASpending, openFDA, and Congress STOCK Act disclosures. Pre-structured, token-optimized market intelligence by vertical. 10x token cost reduction. 18 verticals. Congressional trading data embedded as a cross-signal inside Defense, Pharma, and AI vertical CKGs.
